CAREER PATH

Intern

Interns are given tasks by lead developers. Some of the intern’s responsibilities may include attending conferences, designing and programming, or simply learning things from other developers. These tasks aim to provide them with enough knowledge and experience while having fun learning.

Front-End Engineer

Front-End Engineers are responsible for implementing visual elements that users see and interact within a web application. We are looking for ambitious and self driven Front-End Engineers to join our team. At our company, you will be working with the latest technology and will have the chance to advance and grow your career.

Back-End Engineer

Back-end engineers primarily develop and maintain the core functional logic and operations of a software application or information system. We are looking for a driven and analytical Back-end developer to join our team. If you are looking for a key role in a fast growing company, we want you! As our Back-end Web Developer you will have a big influence on our company's technical platforms.

Project Manager

Manages and oversees all aspects of technology to ensure it is completed on-time and prepares reports for upper management regarding the status of the project. If you are a passionate, inspiring and forward-thinking leader, we have the perfect job for you! We are looking for a competent and experienced Project Manager to provide excellent strategic leadership and assume responsibility for the successful management of our business.
